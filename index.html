About Me

Hi, I'm Phil! I'm a developer based in Tustin, California.

I've always been into building computers and playing videogames since I was young, so I decided to step over the fence to see what the other side was like. Surprisingly, I found it much more addicting, challenging, and rewarding. I'm determined to become a full-stack web developer, passionate about learning new skills, honing/mastering the ones I already have, and look forward to working with you in the future!

When I'm not sitting in front of a computer or working on my projects, you'll probably find me outdoors working on my photography/videography, at the gym, or reading/learning the newest web development trends necessary to stay on top of my game.
